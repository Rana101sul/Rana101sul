Bear

Bears are among the most massive and powerful animals on earth.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years
  • Habitat: They are found in a wide range of habitats in both the Northern and
    Southern Hemispheres,mainly the Americas, Europe and Asia.
  • Species: There are 8 species of bears:
    1. American black bear.
    2. asiatic black bear.
    3. brown bear.
    4. giant panda bear.
    5. polae bear.
    6. spectacled bear.
    7. sloth bear.
    8. sun bear.

Common characteristics of bears include large bodies with stocky legs, long snouts, shaggy hair,
plantigrade paws with five nonretractile claws, and short tails.
They can run very fast and are also good at climbing and swimming.
